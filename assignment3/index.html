Gen Z loves Fast Fashion

Fast Fashion is a problem mainly amoung Gen Z, who are both eco-consious and drawn to affordable trends, being one I can relate. Brands like Shein and Temu offer low-priced clothing that appeal to younger consumers seeking to stay fashionable without overspending.

Although awareness of fast fashions negative impact is growing, studies show that when young people learn about the environmental harm caused by brands like Shein, they are less likely to buy from them. With more people bringing these issues to light, we can reduce the harm.
